On Materialism

As all things, this webpage and myself are products of an interminable chain of causal events and their respective material conditions. What's exhibited here is the manifestation of a childhood spent in a family-built barn in the redwoods, an adolescence inundated with love and support, and a burgeoning adulthood in the last stumbling steps of the leviathan.
