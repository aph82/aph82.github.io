Walk Through
It's nice to start with a materialist conception of world history. There are no heroes, and everything happens for a reason. Seed flesh on bone with an abundance of music. Give time and self to a cause-- money isn't backed by the gold standard anymore. Be indiscriminately concerned with the well-being of all life, not just those organisms most similar to you. Know your class, know your enemy. Saunter towards a world where all scientific knowledge is public. Bring back many souvenirs. Though free will is illusory, your world is mutable; make your mark.
